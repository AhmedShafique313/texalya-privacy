Texalya Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/29/2025   |   Last Updated: 8/28/2025

Texalya (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our services, including our website, AI-powered textile design platform, and related features (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide
- Account details (name, email, password).

b. Automatically Collected Information
- Device info, browser type, IP address, and log data.

c. Generated Content
- Patterns, previews, and outputs generated by our AI models.

We use data to provide and improve our Services, generate textile patterns, process payments, manage subscriptions, communicate updates, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with service providers, legal authorities, or during business transfers (e.g., acquisition).

We implement security measures to protect data. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable precautions to safeguard your information.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as necessary for Services, legal compliance, or dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your account and data.

6. User Rights

You may have rights under data laws: access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and withdrawal of consent. Contact us at info@texalya.ai.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analytics. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Such data will be deleted if discovered.

9. International Users

By using Texalya, you agree that your data may be processed outside your country, including in regions with different data protection laws.
